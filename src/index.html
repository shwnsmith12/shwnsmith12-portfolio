About me

I spent my early years in a cozy town in New England, where I went directly into the workforce after graduating high school. I was working multiple jobs and trying to find what was next when I was offered the chance to move down to Austin, TX to work with family in the real estate business when I was 24. I took it.

After my first year in Austin, working as a personal assistant to an up-and-coming agent, we decided to take a leap of faith and started working on building a new type of brokerage. Along with the broker and owner, I spent the formative years with the company to bring it from start up to an agency that recently closed more than half a billion in sale production in 2021, and employs nearly 100 agents.

While I am proud of everything we have created, I became drawn to the world of Web Development, and all the possibilities that it brought to learn something new every day. This lead me to the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp through the University of Texas. Through this program I have learned the building blocks of HTML, CSS, Javascript, SQL, MongoDB and so much more. I have also independently studied Ruby on Rails, React, and Python. I am genuinely excited to keep building on my burgeoning skills.

I grew up playing sports, but coordination got the better of me before too long. I have always relished working within a team environment, but my "competitive" streak is geared more toward finding ways for everyone to succeed rather than having anyone lose. I've been told on multiple occasions that I would make a good teacher, and I think that it's my favorite compliment.
